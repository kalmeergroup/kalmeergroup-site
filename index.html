Meet our founder

Michelle — Founder, Kalmeer Group

Michelle is a seasoned interview coach, speech consultant, and facilitator with a deep passion for helping people show up as their best selves. With years of experience across recruitment, corporate training, and public speaking, she brings warmth, structure, and sharp insight to every engagement.

Her approach is grounded in one belief: preparation breeds confidence — and confidence opens doors. She has coached executives, graduates, career changers, and returners — adapting her methods to meet each person exactly where they are.

Whether preparing for an exam, finding the confidence to claim the room, developing emerging talent, hosting an important event, or inspiring an audience — Kalmeer creates experiences that leave people feeling seen, supported, and stronger.
