A Kalmeer movement

I see you.

Some people have walked through cancer, brain injury, loss — chapters that would have broken most of us. This movement exists to witness them, honour them, and help them tell their story with the power it deserves. Because being seen changes everything.

The showcase

One story a month, told with care and dignity — approved word for word by its owner before the world ever sees it.

The seat

Every Own Your Story cohort holds one fully funded place for someone rebuilding after illness, injury, or loss — funded by this community.

The evenings

Quarterly live storytelling nights. Storytellers and their people always attend free.

Story 001 — coming soon

The founder goes first. You can't ask people to be seen before you've stood in that light yourself.

Nominate someone

Our promise: every storyteller approves every word. No one we feature ever pays — money flows around this movement, never from its subjects. And support is always within reach: telling a story is powerful, but it isn't therapy, and we help people find professional care whenever it's needed.
